Tankalot

Game

Tankalot is a fun multiplayer game for up to 8 players where the aim is to be the last player alive. Engage in two-player duels or go all out in an eight-player battle, travel across many locales, defeat your friends and be the last tank standing. If that's not your kind of thing. then partake in many singleplayer activities including the bucket list singleplayer campaign and more.
